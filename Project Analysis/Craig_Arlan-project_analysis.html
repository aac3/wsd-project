SITE INTENT

The website redesign intent is to change the coding errors, reorganize content, and provide a web environment that handles the needs of users and school staff. Starting with the navigation that is very crowded and unusable by web standards. The home page lacks presentation and can be a great source for quick information like whats new, newsletters, and uniforms and supply list. The redesign will take the site from 21 pages down to 6 pages. This will organize content and place related information in the same locations. In addition, content on each page will use more space than before. This will help the target audience to find the information they need to most. By providing this redesign, the site will keep the target audience up to date on school information, as well as, provide a site that is easy to navigate and find content that is well organized.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

After doing some research on elementary schools in the area, I recommend that Spring Lake Elementary should improve their section for parents, and students. To be competitive to other elementary schools in the area, providing a section just for parents to give them insight on how to register their child, a list of the curriculum, and a printable page on uniforms and other school supplies. In addition, allowing parents to have information on the administration and faculty staff before enrolling their child will give them knowledge on who to talk to when they arrive on campus. Providing the parents with information like this can be a big boost in student enrollment and more parents visiting the website.

TARGETED AUDIENCE

The target audience is mostly females that are in the Florida area. They are middle-aged mothers and grandmothers. These women are visiting the site mostly from their homes at least 5.8 times a month. In addition, they spend on a monthly average about 5.26 minutes on the site. The target audience uses the site for school schedules, uniform and supply information, and PTA dates. Redesigning the site around this audience will help with more students having the right supplies and uniform, as well as, having more parents at PTA meetings. Providing this features will also boost traffic and time spent on the site.
